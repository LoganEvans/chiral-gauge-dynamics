\title{Chiral Gauge Dynamics: Formally Verified Emergence of Gravity, Quantum Mechanics, and Topological Matter from a \spinFourC Connection}
\author{Logan P. Evans}
\email{evanslp@uw.edu}
\affiliation{University of Washington}
\date{July 11, 2026}

\begin{abstract}
  We present Chiral Gauge Dynamics (CGD), a background-independent formulation of physics derived from a continuous, classical \spinFourC gauge connection. By utilizing the Lean4 formalization language to guarantee mathematical rigor, we present three separate Lorentzian geometries that bypass the reality conditions which historically derailed pure-connection gravity research. We also demonstrate that phenomena often assumed to require quantization (specifically a mass gap, quantum entanglement, and the Born rule) emerge from the classical topology of the field. Finally, we show that a \spinFourC universe requires an isovector axial-vector condensate and show that this condensate is consistent with data measuring the proton radius puzzle.
\end{abstract}
